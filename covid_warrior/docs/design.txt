---------------------------------------------------------------
Design
---------------------------------------------------------------
This document contains the design priorities for the 
Covid Warrior game. They are divided by top, middle 
and low priorities.


---------------------------------------------------------------
Top Priorities
---------------------------------------------------------------
- Game Starts
- Enemies appear
- player appears
- Player can move


---------------------------------------------------------------
Middle Priorities
---------------------------------------------------------------
- enemies Move
- shots fire
- collisions of projectiles and enemies


---------------------------------------------------------------
Low Priorities
---------------------------------------------------------------
- score tracked and displayed
- collision of enemy and player
- end game from win condition
- end game from lose condition
- add sound for collisions
